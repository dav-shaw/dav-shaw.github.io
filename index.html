David Shaw

Hello,

I'm a writer, instructor, and researcher based in Montreal.

My work focuses on the impact of climate change on realist fiction. I've taught classes on essay writing, grammar, and the short story, and published work on Posthumanism, agnotology, and postcolonial theory.

I'm currently the coordinator of the Representing the Anthropocene reading series at Concordia University.
